About Me

I am an undergraduate physics student at Sun Yat-sen University, broadly interested in nonlinear dynamics, complex systems, and AI for science. My recent work focuses on multistability and basins of attraction (geometry, symmetry, basin boundaries), as well as networked dynamical systems such as Kuramoto oscillators (phase-locked states, winding numbers, and stability under dense/long-range coupling).

Education

B.S. (in progress), Physics, Sun Yat-sen University .

Research Interests

Basins of attraction, multistability, equivariant/symmetric dynamics, network synchronization, scientific machine learning.

Current Focus

Computational basin mapping and theory of basin geometry; stability/scaling in Kuramoto-type ring networks.

Tools

Python / Julia for simulations and data analysis; LaTeX for academic writing and reproducible reports.
