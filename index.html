About Me

I am an undergraduate physics student at Sun Yat-sen University (Zhuhai). My interests lie in nonlinear dynamics, complex systems, and AI for science. I currently focus on multistability and basins of attraction (geometry, symmetry, basin boundaries), as well as network synchronization (e.g., Kuramoto oscillators on rings/graphs, winding numbers, and stability under dense or long-range coupling).

I enjoy combining theory with computation, building reproducible simulation pipelines and writing with LaTeX. I’m always happy to discuss research ideas and potential collaborations.
