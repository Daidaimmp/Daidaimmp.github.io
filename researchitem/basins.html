Basins of Attraction & Multistability

Geometry, symmetry, and boundary structure of basins in nonlinear dynamical systems, with an emphasis on multistability and hidden attractors.

Key Questions

  • Hidden attractors 的 basin 边界有哪些可观测的几何特征？
  • 系统对称性如何在 basins 中体现（等变性、群作用、等价类）？
